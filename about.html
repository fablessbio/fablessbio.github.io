About

The fabless model, applied to biology.

fablessBio aims to lead the renaissance of synthetic biology in the bioeconomy — designing biological systems with artificial intelligence and bringing them to the world through partners.

5'— —3'
01

Why fabless

In the semiconductor industry, fabless companies design chips without owning the fabrication plants that make them. Design intelligence and manufacturing are decoupled, and innovation accelerates.

fablessBio brings that model to biology. We concentrate on the part that compounds — the design intelligence — and build genomic and protein foundation models that propose what to make. The building and testing run through a network of academic and industrial partners. Discovery happens in silico, before the bench.

02

An intelligent biomanufacturing platform

Our platform is built to design, model, build, test, and learn (DMBTL) biosystems and technologies for any purpose. Models narrow an enormous search space; experiments confirm the survivors; results train the next generation of models.

fablessBio-AI develops genomic and proteomic large language models that provide new discovery for agriculture, foods, medicines, and materials — the industries that run on biology.
