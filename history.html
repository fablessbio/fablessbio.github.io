History

From a lab bench to foundation models.

fablessBio grew out of decades of computational and synthetic biology research. The timeline below traces that path — and it is still being written.

5'— —3'
2013

Genome-scale foundations

Founders contribute to the 1000 Fungal Genomes Project with the Joint Genome Institute, building the large-scale, annotated sequence base that later trains our models.

2020

fablessBio is founded

Established as a fabless company for biology and affiliated with the Computational & Synthetic Biology Laboratory (CSBL) at Korea University.

2021–2023

The DMBTL platform

Development of an intelligent biomanufacturing platform organized around the Design–Model–Build–Test–Learn loop, with industry and research collaborators.

2024

Genomic and proteomic LLMs

fablessBio-AI advances genomic and protein foundation models for discovery across agriculture, foods, medicines, and materials.

2025–

Training at national scale

Participation in the National GPU Support Program of the Ministry of Science and ICT (MSIT) provides the compute to train and evaluate foundation models at scale.

Dates reflect program milestones. This timeline is maintained as the company grows.
