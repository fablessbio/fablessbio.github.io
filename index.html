01

Design biology with intelligence, not infrastructure.

fablessBio operates an intelligent biomanufacturing platform organized around a single loop — Design, Model, Build, Test, Learn. Models propose; experiments confirm; every result trains the next design. The wet work runs through partners; the design intelligence is ours.

Design & Model

In silico first

Foundation models for DNA, RNA, and protein narrow an enormous search space to a short list of candidates worth making.

Build & Test

Validate with partners

Candidate strains, enzymes, and pathways are built and assayed through academic and industrial collaborators.

Learn

Close the loop

Measured outcomes feed back into the models, compounding accuracy with every cycle.

02

Three foundation models for the code of life.

DNA

Genome foundation model

Genomes are language. We train sequence models that predict genes, annotate function, and surface novel regulatory and coding elements at genome scale.

Protein

Protein foundation model

The protein universe is built from a finite set of folds. Structure-aware models let us design and engineer enzymes and biomolecules toward a target function.

Genomic LLM

Biosequence language model

One representation across DNA, RNA, and protein — a shared model that reasons over biological sequence the way large language models reason over text.
